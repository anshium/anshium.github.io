My Cat and Dog

None of them are with me anymore - just their memories.

They were strays who came to us but we eventually ended up semi-adopting them. We took care of basic food and comfort, gave them a place at our home whenever they wanted to come but were free to go out and explore whatever they want.

They were one of the sweetest. The dog Kalu use to accompany us from the bus stop to my home, sit besides me during thunderstorms, protected us from other dogs.

The cat Cat Billi / Meow Billiwas just as sweet. Didn't like me away even for a minute, sometimes used to sleep besides me and meowed when I went away.
